Signal processing and analysis of human brain potentials (EEG) [Exercise 5]

(This assignment is directly based on the ERP PURSUE Course)

The purpose of this assignment is to develop your ability to read and critically evaluate a journal article that uses ERP techniques to answer a research question. Specifically, you should be able to identify the research question and important elements of the research design, and explain how the ERP data help to answer this question.
